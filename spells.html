Building spells

Build modes

If the default build mode is accidentally changed, it can be selected back by putting the Force into offhand until "Default toggled" is seen in chat:

Adding elements

With the default build mode selected spells are made by either dropping or right-clicking any of the five elements:

The element you clicked is then added to a spell that shows up in your rightmost slot

Adding new lines

Some spells may require multiple lines:

This is achieved by either putting one of the elements or the spell in your offhand

Activating the spell

When pointing to a target you click the spell, causing it to activate within a few seconds
If the spell isn't destructive it can usually be activated again and again
Spells that continue doing somehing (e.g. holding a mob) should not be removed from your inventory

Dropping the spell would cause the creeper to become free

Creating another spell

Spells that are done can be moved out of the rightmost slot:

While the destructive spells are automatically consumed after use some will remain after activating
Unwanted spells thus need to be thrown out
