Build modes

If the default build mode is accidentally changed, it can be selected back by putting the Force into offhand until "Default toggled" is seen in the chat:

Adding elements

In this mode spells are made by either dropping or right-clicking the five elements:

The spell then shows up in your rightmost slot

Adding new lines

Some spells require multiple lines:

This is added by either putting one of the elements or the spell in your offhand

Activating the spell

When pointing to a target you click the spell, causing it to run
If the spell isn't destructive it can be run again
Spells that continue doing somehing (e.g. holding a mob) should not be removed from your inventory

Dropping the spell would cause the creeper to become free

Creating another spell

Spells that are done can be moved out of the rightmost slot:

Unwanted can be thrown out
Destructive spells are automatically consumed upon use, while others will remain in your inventory
